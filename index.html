The Solution: An Intelligent Data Management Cloud

Informatica provides a unified platform to automate data governance and privacy, turning fragmented data into a trusted asset for AI and analytics. This process is built on four key pillars.

1. FIND

Automatically discover and inventory all citizen data assets across every ministry and cloud environment with an AI-powered catalog.

2. UNDERSTAND

Give data business context. Link technical assets to a business glossary and profile data to understand its quality and fitness for use.

3. TRUST

Ensure integrity and compliance. Automatically classify sensitive data, apply masking rules, and monitor quality to build trust.

4. ACCESS

Enable secure, governed data sharing through a self-service marketplace and APIs, empowering agencies and AI applications.
